Your Privacy Matters

Last updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Fit Pulse ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our web application, Progressive Web App, or any related services (collectively, the "Service"). By using Fit Pulse, you agree to the practices described below.

Policy Details

1Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, profile photo, and Google account ID. You may also create custom exercises, workout schedules, and log workout data including weight, repetitions, rest intervals, exercise selection, and session duration.

Information Collected Automatically

We may collect device information such as browser type, operating system, screen resolution, timezone, and general usage statistics (which features are used, app open frequency, error logs). This data is used to improve stability and performance.

Information Stored Locally

If you use Fit Pulse without signing in, all your data — exercises, schedules, workout history, preferences — is stored exclusively on your device via your browser's local storage. We do not have access to this data.

2How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: To provide, maintain, and operate the workout tracking, history, analytics, and sync features.
  • Account Management: To authenticate you, sync your data across devices, and personalize your experience.
  • Improvement: To analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, and develop new features.
  • Communication: To send essential service notifications, security alerts, and policy updates. We do not send marketing emails without explicit consent.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, respond to lawful requests, and protect our rights.

3Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or Switzerland, we process your personal data under the following lawful bases:

  • Consent: For optional features like cloud sync and analytics cookies.
  • Contract: To deliver the Service you requested when you create an account.
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve the Service, prevent abuse, and ensure security.
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with applicable laws and regulations.

4Cookies & Local Storage

Fit Pulse uses browser local storage and session storage to remember your preferences (theme, units, active view) and to cache your offline workout data. We also use authentication cookies provided by our identity provider (Supabase) when you sign in.

Third-Party Cookies

If we display advertisements via Google AdSense or similar advertising networks, those services may set cookies to deliver and measure ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising through Google Ads Settings or aboutads.info.

5Google AdSense & Advertising

Fit Pulse may display advertisements served by Google AdSense or other third-party ad networks. These networks may use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our app or other websites.

  • Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our Service.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visits to Fit Pulse and other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • We comply with Google's AdSense Program Policies and the EU User Consent Policy where applicable.

We do not share personally identifiable information (such as your name or email) with advertising networks.

6Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Authentication and database hosting via Supabase. Authentication via Google OAuth.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, subpoena, court order, or government request.
  • Safety & Rights: To protect our rights, property, safety, or that of our users or the public.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, with notice provided to you.
  • Aggregated Data: Anonymized, aggregated statistics that cannot be linked back to you may be shared publicly.

7Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Workout history is retained indefinitely unless you delete individual sessions or your entire account. When you delete your account, all associated personal data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

8Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Request permanent deletion of your account and data.
  • Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data in certain situations.
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw any consent you previously gave.
  • Complaint: Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

To exercise these rights, email privacy@fitpulsegymtracker.com.

9California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act and California Privacy Rights Act, including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete personal information, the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information, and the right to non-discrimination for exercising these rights. We do not sell your personal information.

10Children's Privacy

Fit Pulse is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

11Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption in transit, encryption at rest, secure authentication tokens, and access controls. However, no system is 100% secure. You are responsible for keeping your account credentials confidential.

12International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your country of residence, including the United States. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses to protect your data during international transfers.

13Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified through the app or via email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this policy indicates when it was most recently revised. Continued use of Fit Pulse after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

14Contact Us

For questions, concerns, or to exercise your privacy rights, contact our Data Protection team at privacy@fitpulsegymtracker.com or visit our Contact page.
