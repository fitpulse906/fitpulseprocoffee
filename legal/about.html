about fit pulse

fit pulse is a fast, offline-first workout tracker built for lifters who care about their training and their privacy. we believe logging a set should take a tap, not a tutorial.

what we do

we make a single, focused tool for the gym. log your sets, time your rest, plan your week, and watch your numbers move over time. no social feeds, no streak-shaming notifications, no upsell every time you open the app.

who we are

fit pulse is built by a small team of lifters and engineers who use the app every day. every feature you see was added because we needed it in our own training. if a feature does not earn its place, it does not ship.

our principles

  • privacy first — your workout data is yours. we do not sell it, rent it, or use it for ad profiling.
  • offline first — the app works without a connection. sync is optional, not required.
  • speed first — every screen is built to load instantly, even on slow networks and older phones.
  • honest first — no dark patterns, no fake scarcity, no hidden paywalls behind basic logging.

how it works

fit pulse is a progressive web app. that means you install it directly from your browser onto any device — phone, tablet, laptop — with no app store in the middle. your data stays on your device by default. if you sign in, it syncs securely so you can pick up your workout on any device you own.

data and privacy

we collect only what is needed to make the app work. if you choose to sign in, we store your exercises, schedule, and session history so they can sync across your devices. you can export or delete this data at any time from your account, or by contacting us directly.

support and feedback

this app gets better because lifters tell us what is broken and what is missing. bug reports, feature ideas, complaints, partnership inquiries, press requests, privacy questions — all of it goes to the same inbox, and a real person reads it.

when reporting a bug, including your device, browser, and a short description of what you were doing helps us fix it faster. for feature requests, telling us the problem you are trying to solve is more useful than telling us the exact feature you want — it lets us think about the best solution.

we aim to reply within 2 business days. privacy and data deletion requests are prioritized.
