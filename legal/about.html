Our Mission

flagWhy Fit Pulse Exists

Fit Pulse was built on a simple belief: tracking your training should be frictionless. No bloated feeds, no paywalls behind basic features, no compromises on your data.

We focus on what actually moves the needle in the gym — logging sets, timing rest, and visualizing your progression over time. Everything else is noise.

Our Principles

lockPrivacy First

Your workout data belongs to you. We never sell it, rent it, or use it for advertising profiling. Local-first by design — sync only if you choose to.

boltPerformance First

Fit Pulse is a Progressive Web App. It loads instantly, works offline, and stays out of your way. No 200MB downloads, no ad-laden onboarding flows.

favoriteBuilt by Lifters, for Lifters

Every feature comes from real gym sessions. We use Fit Pulse ourselves, every single day, and obsess over making the next set easier to log than the last.

Built With

Fit Pulse is crafted with modern web technologies — HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — and powered by Supabase for optional cloud sync. The app is a Progressive Web App, meaning you can install it directly from your browser onto any device, with no app store required.
