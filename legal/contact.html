What to Expect

scheduleResponse Times

We aim to respond to every inquiry within 24 to 72 business hours. Privacy and data deletion requests are prioritized and typically handled within 48 hours, in line with applicable data protection regulations including GDPR and CCPA.

boltUsually within 2 business days

tips_and_updatesBefore You Email

If you're experiencing a bug, please include your device model, browser, and operating system version. Screenshots or short screen recordings drastically help us reproduce and resolve issues faster.

For feature requests, tell us the problem you're trying to solve — not just the feature you want. We'd rather build the right solution than the obvious one.

policyPrivacy Inquiries

You have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data at any time. Email our privacy team with the email address linked to your Fit Pulse account, and we'll process your request promptly.

campaignPress & Media

For interview requests, press kits, or media collaborations, reach out via the partnerships email above with "Press" in the subject line.
