    Train Your Focus
    Daily practice. Hold attention at the edge of clarity and gently sharpen what you see.
    Choose Eye Side
    Step 1 of 2
    Left Eye

    How It Works Look at distant text or an object that is just barely blurry — the "blur edge". Blink softly, relax your eyes, and try to bring it into clearer focus. Stay calm. No straining.
    Quick Guide
    Best Time
    Morning natural light works best. Outdoors > indoors.
    Frequency
    Aim for 1–2 sessions daily. Consistency > duration.
    Per Eye
    If practicing single-eye, do both sides equally.
    Important
    Never strain. Soft focus, soft blink. Stop if uncomfortable.
    Recent Sessions
    Active Focus is a personal practice for visual awareness and attention. It is not a medical treatment. For any vision concerns, consult a qualified optometrist or ophthalmologist.
